who we are

assist, educate, inspire - find out how

The Developer Collective was founded in September 2022 by Renner McCreath with one goal in mind - to help those in need.
Based in Toronto, The Developer Collective is committed to offering volunteer web development services to charities, non-profits, and organizations in need.
